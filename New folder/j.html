Supporters of Solvers

(SOS)



We are supporters of solvers. We do support and help the people who are fond of problem solving. We do provide basic calculator, formula sheet, values of universal constants and aslo unit convertor. By providing these tools, we beleive that we will be able to save a lot of time of solvers which they spend on searching for forgotten formulas, finding the value of constants and doing basic calculations.














We beleive that the life is all about solving problems. There are infinite problems in anyone's life. However, addressing and supporting each of them is beyond the scope of this website. Hence, this website provides support only for scientific problems, mainly the problems students do solve for their regular practice.











We are planning for more facilities also in near future.We hope we were able to provide sufficent help to all the brave solvers who are not ready to give up on any problem. There are endless problems, we wish you luck for your next.



Thank You!
